Zentrix Equity

Real-time Equity & Valuation Platform for Startups and VC Funds.

Visit Live Site Enterprise Ready

Project Overview

Zentrix Equity is a modern fintech platform designed for ZENTRIX LAB to manage complex cap tables, funding rounds, and company valuations in real-time. By utilizing a high-frequency WebSocket-driven architecture, the platform ensures that stakeholders see updates instantly as equity is granted or funding is processed.

The system replaces traditional static spreadsheets with a dynamic, audited ledger that tracks shareholding distribution across multiple rounds. It includes features for team vesting management, document storage, and high-level dashboard analytics for a holistic view of company health.

Technical Highlights

  • ▹Real-Time Updates: Bidirectional communication via Socket.io for instant data synchronization across all clients.
  • ▹Equity Modeling: Automated cap table calculations and dilution modeling for future funding rounds.
  • ▹Secure Architecture: JWT-based authentication and role-based access to protect sensitive financial data.
  • ▹Financial Ledger: Immutable-style event logging of all equity transactions for audit readiness.
